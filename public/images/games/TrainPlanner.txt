名字 ：TrainPlanner
简介：Make your own rail network!
分类：Puzzle

操作方法：
TRAINPLANNER is an open-ended puzzle game where you have to create a safe, efficient and pretty rail network to let Miku deliver her leeks. It's important work!

Please comment your finished tracks, I'd love to see 🩷 (or any bugs oops)

CONTROLS:
Left click to place, right click to delete

PRESS R or use mouse wheel to rotate tracks


Use 1/2/3/4 to switch between different modes, or press the on-screen buttons!


Press T to toggle the UI off if you just wanna watch the trains go
